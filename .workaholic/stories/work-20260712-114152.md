## 2. Motivation

The capability-tryout mission requires every capability to land hermetic-first and then be proven
**live at least once** in an owner-attended session (the `qfs-env-has-live-cloud-accounts` ground
rule: live rounds touch real data and real paid keys, so they are never autonomous). v0.0.59
closed the last implementation gaps that blocked those rounds; this branch is where the rounds
actually ran, their evidence was recorded on the archived tickets, and the defects they surfaced
were captured as tickets before the context was lost.

## 7. Successful Development Patterns

- **Live-first defect discovery.** Driving the shipped binary against real service shapes found
  silent-wrong-answer paths (WHERE-drop, never-fired JOB) that green hermetic tests hid — the
  live round is a distinct, irreplaceable gate, not a rubber stamp.
- **Evidence-on-ticket, defect-as-ticket.** Every round's proof lands on its archived ticket and
  every finding becomes a `todo/` ticket in the same commit, so a knowledge branch merges with a
  complete, resumable backlog and no lost context.
- **Owner-attended write discipline.** Assistant runs PREVIEW + read-back; outward-visible COMMITs
  route through the owner's terminal; self-visible writes are pre-approved. Ten rounds, no
  accidental outward write.
